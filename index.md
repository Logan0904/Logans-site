Backup Power Guide publishes independent buying guides and comparisons for people who want reliable backup power but can't (or don't want to) install a whole-home solar and battery system — renters, apartment dwellers, and small households.

We focus on portable power stations, compact home battery backup units, and energy monitoring tools: what actually matters when choosing one, how the categories differ, and how to avoid overpaying for capacity you don't need.
